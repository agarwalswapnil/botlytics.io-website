This policy applies to all products and services offered by Botlytics.

What type of information we gather

Botlytics service host your bot's statistics on our servers, you must register for an account for the desired services. The registration process asks for your personal information such as but not limited to: Username Password Email address Phone number

How do we use this information

When you log into the Service, our servers record information that your server sends to us. These server logs may include information such as conversation, channel information, team information, platform type, user language, the date and time of conversation. We use these server logs to provide you with analytics and engagement reports.

These individuals are bound by confidentiality obligations and may be subject to discipline, including termination and criminal prosecution, if they fail to meet these obligations.

How we protect your personal information

Botlytics has implemented reasonable security mechanisms to protect Botlytics's Customer Information and Customer User Data that is maintained on Botlytics's servers from loss, misuse and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration and destruction. Examples of these security mechanisms include limited and password-protected access, high security public/private keys, encryption on processed data, and SSL encryption to protect transmission of data. However, please keep in mind that no security system is impenetrable. It may be possible for third parties to intercept or access Botlytics's Customer Information and Customer User Data in spite of these measures. Botlytics cannot guarantee the security of your information and cannot be held responsible for unauthorized access to Botlytics's Customer accounts.

Change to this privacy policy

Botlytics retains the discretion to amend or modify this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes to the way we collect, use or disclose Personally Identifiable Information, we will notify you by posting a clear and prominent announcement on Botlytics or through a direct communication to your Botlytics account.
